Yam and Egg Sauce Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized tuber of yam
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1‚Äì2 fresh peppers (chopped)
  • Vegetable oil (2‚Äì3 tbsp)
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Peel and cut yam into medium chunks. Wash and boil with a little salt until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and fry chopped onions, peppers, and tomatoes until softened.
  3. Add seasoning cube and stir well.
  4. Break eggs into a bowl, whisk, and pour into the tomato sauce.
  5. Stir gently until the eggs are scrambled and well combined with the sauce.
  6. Serve hot yam with the delicious egg sauce.
